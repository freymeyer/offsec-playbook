# My Daily System - Quick Reference

## The 3 Non-Negotiables
1. **Deep Work at 2:15pm** - 90 minutes, one hard thing, no AI shortcuts
2. **Movement at 4pm** - 30 minutes, every day, no excuses
3. **Screens Off by 6:30am** - Sleep quality determines everything

## Daily Schedule
- 2:00pm - Wake, activate, coffee
- 2:15pm - Deep work (90min)
- 4:00pm - Movement (30min)
- 5:00pm - Eat + review
- 6:00pm - Gaming/buffer (timer: 7:50pm)
- 8:00pm - Eat + prep for work
- 10pm-6am - Work shift
- 6:00am - Decompress
- 6:30am - Screens OFF
- 7:00am - In bed

**25/35 minimum** (85% consistency)

## **HOW YOU'LL USE THIS DAILY**

**2:00pm - Wake up:**
- PC boots → Obsidian auto-opens → Today's daily note is already there (auto-generated from template)
- Fill in "One Hard Thing" 
- Start your day

**Throughout the day:**
- Check off boxes as you complete them
- Take notes in "What I Learned" during/after deep work
- If you learn something important, create a permanent note in Study Notes/ and link it

**6:00am - Before bed:**
- Fill in your evening reflection
- Update your score (manually count checkboxes)
- Close Obsidian, sleep

**Sunday 6am:**
- Open Weekly Scorecard
- Tally the week
- Reflect and plan next week
